How it works

Designed for quick iteration: you speak a question, the app trims silence, uploads a compressed audio clip, Whisper transcribes it, and the LLM returns a concise STAR-style answer optimized for spoken use.

1 — Capture & trim

Hold Shift or the on-screen button. The recorder captures raw PCM and uses VAD to trim silence aggressively.

2 — Whisper & LLM

Audio is encoded (Opus/WebM if ffmpeg found) and sent for transcription, then the LLM returns a short STAR-format answer.

3 — Practice

The final answer is shown in the UI — read it out loud and iterate until you're comfortable.
